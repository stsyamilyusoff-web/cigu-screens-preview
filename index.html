Onboarding & Sign-in

The signed-out front door — first-run intro and the ways a teacher gets into the app.

21

Onboarding · Welcome

Signed outFirst run

First screen a brand-new teacher sees. The “cigu” wordmark, a sample RPH card, and the core promise — “RPH siap dalam beberapa minit.” One “Mula” button to start.

22

Onboarding · Choice

Signed outFirst run

Step two: pick how to begin — jump straight in, snap a timetable photo, or sign in. “Cuba dulu tanpa akaun” lets a teacher taste the app with no account.

27

Sign-in

Signed out

Account screen — continue with Apple or Google, or get a passwordless magic-link by email. “Langkau” / “Cuba dulu tanpa akaun” keep it skippable.

28

Magic-link · Error

Signed outSad path

What a teacher sees when a magic-link is invalid or expired — a clear red message and a way back. (The success state redirects straight into the app.)

Hari Ini (Today)

The home tab — today’s teaching agenda built from the timetable.

01

Hari Ini · Signed in

Signed in

The daily home: greeting, an expandable week/month calendar with per-day dots, and the week summary (“21 kelas · 21 perlu RPH”). Shown on a Sunday, so today’s agenda is empty.

23

Hari Ini · Signed out

Signed out

The same tab when no one is signed in — a gentle prompt to go to Profil and log in before the schedule can load.

Arkib (Reuse library)

Find and reuse past activities and reflections instead of generating from scratch.

02

Arkib · Aktiviti

Signed in

The reuse engine: an “Aktiviti / RPH Saya” toggle, search, scope chips, a recommended activity for the next class, and a recent list — each with a one-tap “Guna”.

24

Arkib · Signed out

Signed out

Arkib for a signed-out teacher — the library is gated behind a sign-in notice.

Jana RPH (Generate)

The core action — start a new lesson plan. Reached via the centre ＋ button. Redesigned from a dense form into a one-tap confirm card with an optional 3-step flow behind “Ubah butiran”.

03

Jana RPH · Confirm card

Signed in

The new one-tap default: a single card remembers last week’s lesson (“Diingat dari RPH terakhir” → “Sains · Tahun 4”) so the teacher can just press “Jana RPH”. “Ubah butiran” opens the step-flow to change anything.

33

Step 1 · Subject

Signed inStep flow

Tapping “Ubah butiran” opens step 1 of 3 (“Cikgu ajar apa?”). A searchable subject list grouped by cluster (Lazim / Bahasa / Sains & Matematik), with the current subject (Sains) pre-ticked.

34

Step 2 · Level

Signed inStep flow

Step 2 of 3 (“Tahun berapa?”). Only curriculum-valid levels for the chosen subject are shown — split into Sekolah Rendah and Sekolah Menengah — with the current level pre-selected.

35

Step 3 · Topic

Signed inStep flow

Step 3 of 3 (“Topik apa?”). Live DSKP topic chips for the chosen subject + level, a “Cadangkan untuk saya” suggestion, optional class and advanced settings, and the final “Jana RPH” button.

36

Jana RPH · Signed out

Signed out

The same tab with no account — no form, just a friendly “Cuba tanpa akaun” invite letting a teacher generate one full RPH before signing up.

Kelas · Signed in

25

Kelas · Signed out

The Kelas tab with no session — a sign-in prompt instead of the class grid.
