What changed, and why

  • Real brand lockup. The actual cigu wordmark with the violet spark, not SF-font text. brand presence
  • One accent: violet. Ink text, violet for the payoff word, the chip, the spark, the CTA. No more monotone system-blue. cohesion
  • Sora + Inter type scale. Sora for the wordmark, hero and card title; Inter for body. Deliberate sizes, not defaults. hierarchy
  • 8-pt vertical rhythm. Four grouped blocks (brand, hero, proof, convert) with consistent spacing, not arbitrary gaps. rhythm
  • One alignment. Everything flush-left; the trust row no longer floats centred and orphaned. order
  • No competing banner. "Percuma" is now a quiet line under the single focal CTA, not a blue pill fighting it. focus
